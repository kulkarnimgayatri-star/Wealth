Gmail Configuration

To enable email alerts, please update the config.py file in your project directory with your credentials.

How to find your Email Key?
  1. Enable 2-Step Verification on your Google Account.
  2. Go to your Google Account settings, search for App Passwords.
  3. Select 'Mail' and 'Other (Money Pilot)'.
  4. Generate and copy the 16-character code.
  5. Paste it into config.py as MAIL_PASSWORD.
